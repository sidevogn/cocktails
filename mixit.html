Be Your Own Bartender

Standardization is usually a good thing, but it can lead to a impasse when it comes to innovation. We want to challenge this industry standard with our own MIXIT™ mechanism: Turn the key to connect the liquor with the soda, pull the handle to start the turbofan, twist open the screw cap and enjoy the ride!

Partake In An Experience

We want to provide you with an experience, just like when you open a can of Coke: *sshhh*. Aah, feels good right? We want you to MIXIT together with your friends and share the mesmerizing experience when the two liquids mix into one. And then enjoy the delicious content of course! Have a lovely journey!
